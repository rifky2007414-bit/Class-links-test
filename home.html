About the Technology Stream

Technology Stream is one of the main subject streams in the Sri Lankan G.C.E. Advanced Level (A/L) education system. It was introduced to provide students with practical and theoretical knowledge in technological and engineering-related fields. The Technology stream focuses on practical knowledge in ICT, Engineering, and Applied Sciences. It prepares students for tech-based careers and university education.
